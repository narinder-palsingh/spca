COMPLAINT PROCESS


How to make a complaint


(1) Complainant can make a complaint against the Police Official/Officer in person in the office of State Police Complaint Authority (SPCA) Haryana which is located at OLD PWD (B&R) Building, Sector 19-B, Chandigarh on any working day (as per Haryana Govt. Calendar) during office hours (i.e. 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM).
(2) Complainant or any person on his behalf can make a complaint against the Police Official/Officer by post
(3) Complainant can make a complaint against the Police Official/Officer through E-Mail on address: spca[dot]haryana[at]nic[dot]in

FOR THE CONVENIENCE OF THE COMPLAINANTS


Electronic Complaint will also be entertained and the complaint can be sent at spca[dot]haryana[at]nic[dot]in subject to the sending of supporting affidavit as given as FORM-II alongwith ID and residential proof duly attested by the Gazetted Officer/notary public within a week’s time from the date of sending the complaint; so that anonymous complaints are not generated.
The Complainant can send his/her complaint in the following format which is given as FORM – I and the complaint must be supported by an affidavit in the following format as given as Form-II.
